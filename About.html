About Me

I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student at the University of Colorado - Boulder. My research interests include personal/home security, cyber education, and Zero Trust networks. When not studying, I spend most of my free time biking the local trails or hiking with the family. When the weather is bad....videogames. I always like to help and seek opportunities to serve. In that capacity I have helped lead a CU Boulder cyber team to win the regional Cyberforce competion and have served as a team mentor for the Designing for Defense program. If there is one class I recommend everyone take, it is the CU Boulder D4D class. Does not matter what your major is, take it.

Military Biography


LTC Olson is a seventeen year active duty veteran currently on assignment to the University of Colorado at Boulder, CO. 

He was commissioned as a second lieutenant in the Engineer branch after graduating from the United States Military Academy at West Point, NY in 2004. Early in his career LTC Olson served as a Platoon Leader and Executive Officer for the 320th Engineer Company in Hanau, Germany and Victory Base, Iraq. He then served as the Aide-de-Camp for the Theater Engineer Commander, 130th Engineer Brigade, Balad, Iraq.  After completing his transfer to Functional Area 24 (now 26A) he immediately served as the Senior Network Engineer for 1st Infantry Division, Ft. Riley Kansas and Basrah, Iraq. During this role he oversaw the satellite and long haul network architectures for southern Iraq and lead communication planning efforts for the reconstruction of the Port of Basrah.  He has taught at the United States Military Academy as an Assistant Professor within the Computer Science and Electrical Engineering Departments. In his most recent duty, LTC Olson served as the Senior Network Engineer and Operations Officer for the I Corps G6 where he oversaw the operation, maintenance and integration of Pacific networks with international partners.

He holds master's degrees in Electrical Engineering from the University of Missouri at Columbia, MO, Environmental Engineering from the University of Missouri at Rolla, MO, and Information Technology Management from the University of Maryland University College at Adelphi, MD. He is a graduate of the Engineer Officer Basic Course, Engineer Officer Advance Course,  Network Systems Engineer Course, Army Cyberspace Operations Planners Course, and the Army Command and General Staff College.

LTC Olson holds  the CISSP, CISM, C|CISO, CCNA, and CCIH professional certifications.

LTC Olson's military decorations include: Bronze Star Medal, Meritorious Service Medal (one oak leaf cluster), the Army Commendation Medal (three oak leaf clusters), the Army Achievement Medal, the National Defense Service Medal, the Korea Defense Service Medal, the Global War on Terrism Service Medal, and the Parachutist Badge.
